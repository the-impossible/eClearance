About Project

The aim of this study is to develop an online clearance system for graduating students at Kaduna polytechnic. This research work would include an investigation of the current methods used in clearance, and an evaluation of the feasibility and potential benefits of implementing an online clearance system. The research work will only be cleared for graduating students and not departmental clearance

Our Objectives

The aim of this study is to develop an online clearance system for graduating students at Kaduna polytechnic.

Objective 1

To review the related literature on the online clearance system for graduating students at Kaduna polytechnic

Objective 2

To design or develop an online clearance system to meet graduate clearance needs
